Number 21

17/08/2025

You may be asking, "Why are you doing this one out of order?", I am doing it out of order because my Grandpa wanted to do a walk with me on this weekend and his bus is the number 21. I met with him at Beauchamp street, in Karori. One of the nice things about Karori is that you can see and hear (they're quite loud) a lot of kākā from Zealandia.

Once we got to Kelburn, we sat down at a bakery, I had a cinamon roll, which was quite nice.

The rest of the walk was pretty uneventful as we walked out of Kelburn towards courtney place, where the line ends

Distance: 8.2km
